date_photo: Date on which the dorsal and lateral photos were taken (days after the year 1900)
mayflies: Number of mayflies  (1 per row)
date_sample: Date on which the mayflies were samples
location: Location (Hierd0 = upstream section; Hierd4 = downstream section)
transect: Transect (1 = most upstream transect; 5  = most downstream)
distance: Distance from the stream (0, 25, 50, 100, or 150 m)
ind_code: Unique individual code, expressed as <location_transect_distance_date_count>
sex: Sex (m  = male; f = female; NA = unknown)
life_stage: Life stage (Imago or Subimago)
body_length: Body length (mm), usually taken from dorsal photo
body_length_reliability: Reliability of the measurement (1 = reliable; 2 = less reliable; NA = no photo)
dry_weight: Dry weight (mg)
wing_area: Wing area (m2), usually taken from lateral photo
wing_reliability: Reliability of the measurement (1 = reliable; 2 = less reliable; 3 = unreliable; NA = no photo)
pronotum_width: Pronotum width (mm), usually taken from dorsal photo
mesonotum_width: Mesonotum width (mm), usually taken from dorsal photo
pronotum_mesonotum_reliability: Reliability of the measurement (1 = reliable; 2 = less reliable; NA = no photo)
